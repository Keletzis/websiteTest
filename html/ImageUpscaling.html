Deep learning for image upscaling technology

Introduction

Image and video upscaling is one of many tasks that AI has gotten access in order to enhance, create and understand. Especially older content which cannot be recreated today such as films, shows and even old paintings – pictures can simply be upscaled using Artificial Intelligence. Bringing life to old entertainment can greatly benefit the longevity of the content as well as give the modern consumer a more enjoyable experience.

Figure: SRGEN Image Upscaler[Cp]

Starting with image upscaling, using its selected databases the program analyzes every object whether it is a face, a bottle, a barrel or any different object. It stores that information and given an old or even low-resolution image it can detect different patterns and upscale it accordingly [Source]. Removing objects from images is also not out of the question. The algorithm maps the reference image and of course using the database can calculate what’s in the background and remove the object while filling in the blanks [Source]. Background removal, pose transferring have all been introduced in recent years as well [Source]. Denoising is also a huge innovation that uses AI as its base. Especially older images – films greatly benefit from this.

Video upscaling can work in a similar way as it can split the video in frames and upscale its image individually. Animation is a great example of how the algorithms excel at. By using the databases, AI can calculate the motion of the object shown and animate the image accordingly[Source].

Figure: Old image restoration[Cp]

Data retrieval is also important. Restoring color, lines and even entire frames from older films can now be possible.
