What is role of 4th Industrial Revolution in Education?

A little more explanation why the 4th revolution brought a lot of benefits to Education industry

Mental health of society

Higher education is a mysterious, logical, and dynamic open door in the fourth industrial revolution (HE 4.0) that has the potential to improve society. The fourth industrial revolution will change the workplace from assignment-based qualities to human-focused characteristics

Education

The combination of man and machine will minimize the gap between humanities and sociology and science and innovation. For instance, "celebrity robots" served customers at a restaurant in place of waiters and waitresses. Imagine a world where a monotonous work is not performed by a person, but performed by robots or some kind of device that will help to automate daily processes
Usage of new abilities

Organizations must understand how to use knowledge management concepts to increase the effectiveness of their systems and processes if they are to consider knowledge management as a strategy. The IR 4.0 altered public perceptions about educational technology. In response to the quick advancement of information, a new model of education has been developed for the future. The Fourth Industrial Revolution (the "4th IR") stands out due to its pace, technical fusion, breadth, depth, and return to scale.
