Education 4.0?

1

Numerous changes in education are a result of the fourth industrial revolution. The need for new skills in the 4IR workforce is forcing the education sector to innovate and get ready to create the necessary knowledge. The applications of human-machine interaction are expanded by the management of 4IR through digital physical frameworks and artificial intelligence. Education 4.0 is a new educational approach that is future-focused as a result of a swift innovation revolution .

2

The new educational model pushes students to acquire the necessary competencies as well as knowledge on how to access these resources (Fisk, 2017). In education 4.0, instructors serve as facilitators and data-based personalisation is used to track student development.
