Fourth Industrial Revolution

Introduction

This website is mainly focused on the fourth industrial revolution and it's influence in the future of our society in a variety of fields. We have been living in the fourth industrial revolution for the past couple of years and with the use of Artificial Intelligence, humans aim to maximise the benefits of the last three industrial revolutions in many fields such as the economy, education, medicine as well as many others that we are going to be discussing.
